People and Planet Care Foundation

Committed to climate, environment, biodiversity, and social harmony

Introduction

People and Planet Care Foundation is a non-profit organization voluntarily established by a group of like-minded professional friends based in Zurich, Switzerland. Initially, the foundation focuses on addressing challenges in Switzerland, with plans to gradually extend support to EU countries and poor and climate-affected regions globally.

Structure

Our foundation operates with a core team of volunteers, environmental experts, and social activists who collaborate to design and implement projects focused on environmental protection and social development.

Vision

To create a world where people and nature coexist in harmony, ensuring a healthy planet and equitable society for future generations.

Mission

To actively promote the protection of climate, biodiversity, glaciers, and wildlife while fostering peace, social cohesion, and equality among all communities.

10 Key Objectives Aligned with UNSDGs

  • Protect and restore biodiversity and wildlife habitats.
  • Combat global warming and promote renewable energy.
  • Preserve glaciers and natural water sources.
  • Enhance peace and social cohesion while countering hate and extremism.
  • Promote gender equality, women, child, and youth development.
  • Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education.
  • End gender-based violence and discrimination.
  • Support climate adaptation efforts in vulnerable communities.
  • Foster sustainable agriculture and reduce environmental pollution.
  • Encourage global cooperation on environmental and social challenges.

Focus Areas

We concentrate on issues affecting both people and planet, including: climate and environment protection, biodiversity and wildlife conservation, glacier preservation, combating global warming, promoting peace, countering hate and religious extremism, and supporting gender equality and social development.

Geographic Focus

Starting from Switzerland and the European Union countries, our foundation plans to expand its outreach to poor and climate-affected countries, assisting the most vulnerable populations.
